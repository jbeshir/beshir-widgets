# Goblin Courier — mounted design contract

Goblin Courier is a bounded top-down action run across 10 authored templates instantiated as 11 rooms: Threshold, Gallery, Junction A, safe Antechamber or fast Barracks, Junction B, optional Storeroom, Cracked Hall, Shrine Approach, Warden Arena, and Delivery. Combat rooms are gated. The safe route costs a lever puzzle and rewards a chest; the fast route exposes four enemies and skips that reward. Storeroom supplies the second possible chest and a rune that exchanges Instability for Toss recharge.

Enemies are frame-driven: Grunts patrol and telegraph lunges, Weavers drift and telegraph harmful enemy-owned bolts, Sentinels guard and telegraph charges that Brace can parry, and the Warden has HP-keyed sweep/slam, volley, and rune-shelter surge phases. Dash/Toss recharge, telegraphs, hazards, burns, invulnerability, rune charging, and overloads are simulation-frame counters; no gameplay timer uses wall time. All six deterministic upgrades have runtime effects and at most two chest selections occur in a route.

Logical keyboard inputs are up/down/left/right (arrows or WASD), dash (Space), toss (F), brace (J), interact (E), pause (Escape), and mute (M). Pointer and TouchEvent routes share ID-aware movement/brace maps; releasing or cancelling one ID cannot clear another. Production boot is `#widget-ready` attached with visible `canvas#game-canvas` and enabled `#start-button`. Production core movement reveals numeric `#hp-value`; core interact changes the canvas accessible label to Dust Gallery. Test builds alone expose `#game-ready`, `data-game-state`, `data-testid`, and `window.__game` queries/setup methods.

Reduced motion follows the media query and explicit persisted toggle, removes pulsing edge motion, and retains solid meter bands plus static phase/pattern labels. Mute, reduced motion, best score, and deliveries persist safely. A lifecycle-owned ResizeObserver posts `{type:'widget-resize', widget:'goblin-courier', width, height}` and disconnects on unmount.
