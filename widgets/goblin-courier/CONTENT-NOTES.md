# Content notes

Goblin Courier contains mild, stylized fantasy combat against pixel-art dungeon creatures, visible health loss, traps, magical projectiles, and a non-graphic defeat state. There is no blood, gore, horror imagery, substance use, sexual content, or realistic violence.

Package overloads use brief color pulses and code-drawn effects. The in-game reduced-motion toggle and the system `prefers-reduced-motion` preference suppress or simplify motion-heavy feedback.
